    Quito

    Quito, Ecuador's capital, is a city that feels both historic and alive. Nestled high in the Andes, it charms visitors with its colonial Old Town full of plazas, churches, and cobblestone streets. At the same time, modern neighborhoods buzz with cafes, museums, and local markets. The city is framed by stunning volcanoes like Cotopaxi and Pichincha, giving it a dramatic backdrop. From El Panecillo's hilltop views to the famous Middle of the World monument, Quito blends culture, adventure, and breathtaking scenery.
